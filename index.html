American service

where the service depends on the host who takes orders from customers, then sends them to the kitchen, which prepares them in dishes for each category, decorates them artistically, and sends them to the dining halls by the hosts or their assistants, as they carry them to the customers' tables.

Russian service

where the chefs inside the kitchen prepare the food, decorate it and cut it artistically, and place it on silver trays called serving trays, and the hosts deliver Food for customersâ€™ tables, and then presenting it to them for their approval before serving it to them

English service

This system is known as the family service system, and it is not very different from the American system, as it depends on the trays that the chefs prepare and prepare the food on in a professional manner.

Counter service

It is the system used in fast food restaurants. This system depends on the speed and skill of the host in taking orders, sending them to the kitchen, preparing ready-made drinks behind the counter
